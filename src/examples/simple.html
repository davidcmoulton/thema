Stencila recognises lists in Google Docs including unordered (bulleted) lists like this:

  • Apple

  • Pear

  • Peach

And ordered lists like this:

  1. Butter

  2. Cheese

  3. Milk

Nested lists are supported too:

  1. Fruit

    1. Apple

    2. Pear

    3. Peach

  2. Dairy

    1. Butter

    2. Cheese

    3. Milk
